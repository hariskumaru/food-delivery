Instructions

  1. Wash and soak idli rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately for 4-5 hours.
  2. Grind the soaked urad dal and fenugreek seeds together to a smooth batter. Add water as needed and salt to taste.
  3. Grind the soaked idli rice separately to a slightly coarse batter. Add water as needed and mix it with the urad dal batter.
  4. Ferment the batter overnight or for 8-10 hours until it doubles in volume.
  5. Grease the idli plates with oil and pour the batter into each mold.
  6. Steam the idlis in a steamer for about 10-12 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  7. Remove the idlis from the molds and serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
