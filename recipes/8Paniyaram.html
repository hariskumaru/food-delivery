Paniyaram

+ Add to Favorites

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix the idli or dosa batter with chopped onions, green chilies, grated ginger, curry leaves, and salt.
  2. Heat a paniyaram pan and add a few drops of oil in each cavity.
  3. Pour the batter into each cavity, filling them about 3/4th full.
  4. Cook on medium heat until the bottom turns golden brown.
  5. Flip the paniyarams using a spoon or skewer and cook the other side until golden brown.
  6. Remove from the pan and serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
