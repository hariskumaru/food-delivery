Kothu Parotta

+ Add to Favorites

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add chopped onions. Sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes, green chilies, curry leaves, and salt. Cook until the tomatoes turn mushy.
  4. Add red chili powder, turmeric powder, and garam masala. Mix well.
  5. Add the shredded parotta pieces and mix everything together.
  6. Cook for a few minutes until the parotta absorbs the flavors and becomes slightly crispy.
  7. Serve hot with raita or salna.
