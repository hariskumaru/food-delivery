Masala Dosa

+ Add to Favorites

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak rice, urad dal, chana dal, and fenugreek seeds together for 4-5 hours.
  2. Grind the soaked ingredients along with poha to a smooth batter. Add water as needed and salt to taste.
  3. Ferment the batter overnight or for 8-10 hours until it doubles in volume.
  4. Heat a dosa tawa (griddle) and pour a ladleful of batter in the center.
  5. Spread the batter in a circular motion to make a thin dosa. Drizzle oil around the edges.
  6. Cook until the dosa turns golden brown. Flip and cook the other side as well.
  7. Place a portion of potato masala in the center of the dosa and fold it.
  8. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
