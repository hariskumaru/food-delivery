Paneer Butter Masala

+ Add to Favorites

Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a pan and add chopped onions. Sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  3. Add tomato puree and cook until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Grind soaked cashews with milk to a smooth paste and add it to the pan.
  5. Add red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, kasuri methi, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Add paneer cubes and cook until they absorb the flavors.
  7. Stir in fresh cream and simmer for a few minutes.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.
