Grilled Tandoori Chicken

+ Add to Favorites

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix plain yogurt, tandoori masala, lemon juice, vegetable oil, minced garlic, grated ginger, ground cumin, ground coriander, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper.
  2. Make shallow cuts on the chicken thighs to allow the marinade to penetrate.
  3. Coat the chicken thighs with the tandoori marinade, making sure to get it into the cuts.
  4. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes or up to 24 hours for the best flavor.
  5. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  6. Remove the chicken from the marinade and shake off any excess.
  7. Grill the chicken thighs for about 5-6 minutes per side or until they are fully cooked and have beautiful grill marks.
  8. Serve the grilled tandoori chicken with lemon wedges and fresh cilantro on the side.
