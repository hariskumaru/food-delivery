Chettinad Chicken

+ Add to Favorites

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté the chopped onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy.
  4. Add Chettinad masala powder, curry leaves, and salt. Cook for a few minutes.
  5. Add chicken pieces and mix well until they are coated with the masala.
  6. Cover and cook on medium heat until the chicken is cooked through and tender.
  7. Squeeze the soaked tamarind and add the tamarind water to the pan. Simmer for a few more minutes.
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice or roti.
