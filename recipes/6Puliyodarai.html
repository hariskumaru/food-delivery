Puliyodarai (Tamarind Rice)

+ Add to Favorites

Instructions

  1. Cook rice and let it cool.
  2. In a pan, heat sesame oil and add mustard seeds. Let them splutter.
  3. Add chana dal, urad dal, asafoetida, and curry leaves. Saut√© until the dals turn golden brown.
  4. Add tamarind pulp, Puliyodarai powder, and salt. Cook until the mixture thickens.
  5. Add the cooked rice to the pan and mix well, ensuring the rice is coated evenly with the tamarind mixture.
  6. Garnish with roasted peanuts. Serve at room temperature.
